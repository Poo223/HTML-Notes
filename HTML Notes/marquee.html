Marquee tag of HTML:

  • This tag is used to create scrolling texts or image in the webpage.
  • Marquee tag is paired tag.
  • Marquee tag will scrolling specific direction and specified behaviour.

Attribute of < Marquee >< /Marquee > tag:

  • Behaviour=" "(scroll,slide,alternate)
  • Direction=" "(right,left,up,down)
  • Scrollamount=" "(Value speed)
  • Height=" " and Width=" "

Ex:
< marquee behavior="alternate" direction="right" slideamount="10">
